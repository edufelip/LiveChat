# Settings Requirements

## R-032: Profile Editing
The system shall allow users to update their Display Name, Status, and Profile Email.

## R-033: Email Verification
Any change to the user's email address shall require verification of the new email before being finalized.

## R-034: Theme Modes
The app shall support Light, Dark, and System (Auto) theme modes.

## R-035: Block List
The system shall allow users to block other users, preventing them from sending messages or seeing the user's presence.

## R-036: Secure Deletion
Account deletion shall require a recent login session; if the session is expired, re-authentication is mandatory.

## R-037: Privacy Toggles
Users shall be able to toggle "Read Receipts" and "Last Seen" visibility.
